A buzz of Information


During my first year of studies, we where asked to produce a infographic piece, based around the issue of declining bees. Bees are a vital part of our ecosystem, so therefore it is important to sustain the bee population. I based my infographic piece around the fact that the bees contribute a lot to the food production - due to their pollination process.

Claustrophobia


During my second year, we where asked to produce a typographic piece based around phobias - which in my case I chose Claustrophobia (The fear of enclosed spaces.) For my typographic piece, I took inspiration from mazes, as mazes are often confusing to navigate and very hard to escape to.

The Empty Space Project - Design Activism


During my second year, we were put into a team of 4 people, and our assignment was to pick an ongoing issue (Design Activism) and to produce a motion piece to coincide with the website based around that issue. The motion piece must be a call to action for people to visit the website that we created afterwards. Our chosen topic was homelessness - and our aim was to change people's perception of homeless people, and to encourage them to take an action.

You can visit the website that we've created here at: theemptyspace.uk
